Our Objectives

  • To establish the equal constitutional and human rights of transgender ,Hijra & MSM( Men having sex white men) people and to promote awareness of their rights within these communities as well as mainstream society.

Activities of Dinajpur Notun Aalo Society


DNAS takes a tripartite approach to its work, dividing its activities between advocacy, Network Building, and Service Delivery. Through Advocacy, it seeks to counter discrimination and improve the quality of life of PLHIV.



About Dhinajpur Notun Aalo Society

Dinajpur Notun Aalo Society envisions a society where people are no longer discriminated on the basis of gender identity or sexual orientation.Our vision is of a society where all people ,regardless of gender or sexuality ,are able to access all social and economic opportunities necessary for their wellbeing and full development.
