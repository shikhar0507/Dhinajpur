About Dinajpur Notun Aalo Society

Dinajpur Notun Aalo Society envisions a society where people are no longer discriminated on the basis of gender identity or sexual orientation.Our vision is of a society where all people ,regardless of gender or sexuality ,are able to access all social and economic opportunities necessary for their wellbeing and full development.
